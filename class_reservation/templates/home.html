About Natalia Luna

I’m a Mexican Chef with a lifelong passion for food, especially hot and spicy dishes. I’ve had the opportunity to live all around Mexico, from the north to the south, coast to coast.
I’ve always been in the kitchen, cooking with and for both local and foreign palates, and that’s how I’ve learned most of my recipes. Additionally, I pursued studies at the National School of Mexican Gastronomy and L’Académie Culinaire de France. This journey began at Airbnb, where I rapidly became one of the top-rated experiences worldwide.
My success was such that soon I realized that I needed to take Mexican Salsas Experience further. Now you can take my cooking classes anywhere in the world in Spanish, English and French.
I’ve collaborated with companies such as TikTok, Coca-Cola, Facebook, Spotify and many more. My guests frequently come back, refer me to friends, family and coworkers. We are a community that loves Mexican food and shares tips and recipes.
My cooking classes have garnered over 1k 5-star reviews and have been listed among the 20 Best cooking experiences in Latin America by Buzzfeed. It would be my joy to share my love for Mexican food with you.
